TradeCoach

The Base Discipline Agent
An AI trading discipline coach that lives in Base App chat, watches your wallet on-chain, and calls you out the moment you break your own trading rules. It never tells you what to buy. Refusing to — that's the whole point.

Why this exists

I lost $300K to leveraged trading.

Not in one bad trade — in a pattern. A loss, then a bigger position to win it back. Another loss, more leverage. Trading at 2 AM. Re-entering minutes after getting liquidated, because "I still had backup." That backup feeling is the most expensive lie in trading — the account that feels like a cushion today is the account that's gone tomorrow.

When it was over, I understood: my problem was never strategy. It was psychology. And every symptom of trading psychology is visible on-chain — the rapid-fire trades, the size escalation after losses, the late-night entries, the revenge re-entry. It's all in the wallet history, timestamped. So I built the thing I needed and didn't have.

How it works

You trade on Avantis / Aerodrome / Uniswap ↓ Everything lands on the Base blockchain ↓ The agent reads the chain every 60 seconds, computes real USD values ↓ Runs 10 behavior rules against YOUR plan — "did they break their own rule?" ↓ Rule broken → signal fires (same signal max once per 4h — never spam) ↓ A short, direct message hits your Base App DM (encrypted via XMTP)

The non-negotiable design rule: deterministic code decides when to message — never the AI. The AI only picks the words. The agent can't get moody, can't overreach, can't spam.

The Discipline Score (0–100)

A rolling 14-day report card of plan adherence. Everyone starts at 100; violations subtract (liquidation −25, revenge re-entry −20, oversized trade −10…). Send score, get a shareable card. This later becomes an API — imagine perps platforms seeing which traders are disciplined enough to last.

Using it — 30 seconds

DM tradecoach.base.eth in Base App: 0xYourWallet → Step 1 ✓ (just paste your address) set trades 5 → Step 2 ✓ (your daily trade limit) set size 500 → Step 3 ✓ (your max position, USD) Then trade like you normally do. You'll only hear from it when you break your own plan. Commands: status · score · rules · stop / resume Ask "should I buy ETH?" — it refuses. Every time.
